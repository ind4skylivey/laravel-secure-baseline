# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

- Email: livey_sh13lds1b33@keemail.me
- Please include: description, steps to reproduce, impact, and any suggested fix.
- Do **not** open public issues for vulnerabilities.

## Response Targets

- Acknowledgment: within 24 hours
- Initial assessment: within 3 business days
- Fix timeline (severity-dependent): critical within 7 days when feasible

## Scope

- Repository: ind4skylivey/laravel-secure-baseline
- Releases and tags under BSL 1.1

## Safe Harbor

Good-faith research and reporting will not be subject to legal action. Avoid testing against production systems you do not control.
